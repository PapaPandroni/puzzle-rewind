Puzzle Rewind

Turn your own Lichess games into an endless puzzle stream.

What is Puzzle Rewind?

Puzzle Rewind mines a Lichess player's game history for blunders and turns them into an interactive puzzle stream. Instead of solving puzzles from strangers' games, you train on the exact mistakes you made yourself — the fastest way to stop repeating them.

FAQ

Do I need an account?

No. Enter any public Lichess username and start solving. Nothing is stored about you.

Which games are used?

Your recent Lichess games are analyzed for moves that caused a large drop in your win probability. Each blunder becomes a puzzle: find the move you should have played.

Why don't I see puzzles from all my games?

Games that Lichess has already analyzed turn into puzzles instantly. For the rest, we run our own Stockfish engine in the background — up to 40 games per search, within a fair daily budget that keeps this free service free. Those puzzles appear as soon as the engine finishes; if a big backlog hits the daily limit, search again tomorrow and it picks up where it left off.

Why does analysis take a while?

Every search shares one Stockfish engine that works through games one at a time, and as a free service we cap how much analysis runs each day. A progress banner shows how far along your games are, and puzzles appear the moment they're done — keep solving in the meantime. If you'd like analysis to get faster, the little coffee button in the corner genuinely helps pay for more engine time.

Is it free?

Yes, completely free and open source.

Does it work for any Lichess player?

Yes — you can enter any public username, including your friends' (or your rivals').
